"Spooked Out!" is the game my group and I made for CLE4 of the HR CMGT course. It is a togue-like RPG game with randomly put together rooms inn it you assume the role of Sjaak, a ghost hunter sent on his last mission; to remove all of the ghosts haunting the Schiecentrale.

I did the programming for the menu screens aswell as most of the room programming. The lore is also mostly thought up by myself with some spitballing with the other people in my group.
